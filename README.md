## Why These Projects

Most networking portfolios on GitHub are generic. VLANs on a school network. OSPF between three unnamed routers. I wanted mine to be different.

Every project here is tied to a specific industry and a specific business problem. The bank branch project exists because Nigerian banks are legally required to isolate their ATM infrastructure from teller systems. The inter-bank settlement project exists because NIBSS and the CBN mandate how commercial banks connect to the national settlement network. The trading floor project exists because a one-second network delay in a brokerage firm can mean real financial loss.

I want anyone reading this repo to come away understanding not just what I configured, but why it matters in the real world.
